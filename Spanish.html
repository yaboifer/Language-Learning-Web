Pronunciation Tips:

In Spanish, pronunciation is generally straightforward, but here are some tips to help you sound more natural:

  • R sounds: The 'r' in Spanish can be rolled or tapped lightly, depending on its position in the word.

  • Stress and vowels: Stress usually falls on the second-to-last syllable unless there is an accent mark. Vowels are pronounced clearly and consistently.

    • casa → CA-sa
    • comer → co-MER

  • Vowel combinations:

    • AI is pronounced like "eye" (e.g., baile → BAI-le).
    • UE is pronounced like "weh" (e.g., bueno → BWE-no).

  • Consonants:

    • LL is pronounced like the 'y' in "yes" (e.g., llamar → ya-MAR).
    • Ñ is pronounced like the 'ny' in "canyon" (e.g., niño → NEE-nyo).
