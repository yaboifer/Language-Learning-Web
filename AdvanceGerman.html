German for Beginners

Welcome to German for Beginners!

This webpage is designed for beginners who want to learn essential German phrases. You will find common phrases with audio that you can practice. Click the audio buttons to hear the correct pronunciation!

Common Phrases:

1. Hallo - Hello

2. Guten Morgen - Good Morning

3. Guten Tag - Good day

4. Guten Abend - Good evening

5. Entschuldigung - Excuse me

6. Danke - Thank you

7. Guten Nacht - Good night

8. Ja - Yes

9. Nein - No

10. Wo ist...? - Where is...?

Pronunciation Tips:

Learning to pronounce German might seem tricky at first, but it becomes easier once you get familiar with the basic sounds.

  • Vowels

    • A = “ah” like in father → Apfel = "AHP-fel"
    • E = “eh” like in bed → gehen = "GEH-hen"
    • I = “ee” like in see → Ich = "iCH"
    • O = “oh” like in go → Brot = "broht"
    • U = “oo” like in boot Guten → “GOO-ten”

    • Practice slowly and exaggerate the sounds at first. German is a very clear language, every letter is usually pronounced.
