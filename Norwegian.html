Common Phrases:

1. Hei - Hello

2. Takk - Thank you

3. Ha det - Goodbye

4. Vær så snill - Please

5. Unnskyld - Excuse me / Sorry

6. God morgen - Good morning

7. God natt - Good night

8. Hvordan har du det? - How are you?

9. Jeg heter... - My name is...

10. Hvor er toalettet? - Where is the bathroom?

Pronunciation Tips:

In Norwegian, pronunciation can be straightforward with a few key tips to help you sound more natural.

  • R sounds: The 'r' in Norwegian is often rolled lightly with the tip of your tongue, similar to Spanish or Italian.

  • Stress and vowels: Stress usually falls on the first syllable of a word, and vowels are pronounced clearly and distinctly.

    • takk → TAHK
    • hei → HEI (like "hi")

  • Vowel combinations:

    • Æ is pronounced like the 'a' in "cat".
    • Ø is pronounced like the 'u' in "burn" (with rounded lips).
    • Å is pronounced like the 'o' in "more".

  • Consonants:

    • K before 'i' or 'e' is pronounced like "sh" (e.g., kirke → SHIR-ke).
    • G before 'i' or 'e' is soft, like the 'y' in "yes" (e.g., gi → yi).
