Indonesian for Beginners

Welcome to Indonesian for Beginners!

In this section, we will cover some basic Indonesian phrases that are useful for everyday conversations. Listen to the pronunciation by clicking on the audio buttons next to each phrase.

Basic Phrases:

1. Halo - Hello

2. Apa kabar? - How are you?

3. Selamat pagi - Good morning

4. Terima kasih - Thank you

5. Tolong - Please

6. Sampai jumpa - Goodbye

7. Berapa umurmu? - How old are you?

8. Nama saya... - My name is...

9. Di mana kamar mandi? - Where is the bathroom?

10. Berapa harganya? - How much does it cost?

Pronunciation Tips:

In Indonesian, pronunciation is straightforward as it is a phonetic language, but it is still important to pronounce the words clearly. Here are some tips:


  • Stress - In Indonesian, the stress is usually on the penultimate (second-to-last) syllable of the word.

  • Vowels - Vowels are pronounced consistently in all words.

Additional Vocabulary:


  • Ya - Yes

  • Tidak - No

  • Apa waktu sekarang? - What time is it?

  • Maaf - I'm sorry

  • Siapa nama Anda? - What is your name?

  • Saya senang bertemu dengan Anda - Nice to meet you
