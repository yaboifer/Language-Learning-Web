Arabic for Beginners

Welcome to Arabic for Beginners!

In this section, we will cover some basic Arabic phrases that are useful for everyday conversations. Listen to the pronunciation by clicking on the audio buttons next to each phrase.

Basic Phrases:

1. مرحباً - Hello

2. كيف حالك؟ - How are you?

3. صباح الخير - Good morning

4. شكراً - Thank you

5. من فضلك - Please

6. وداعاً - Goodbye

7. كم عمرك؟ - How old are you?

8. اسمي... - My name is...

9. أين الحمام؟ - Where is the bathroom?

10. كم السعر؟ - How much does it cost?

Pronunciation Tips:

In Arabic, the pronunciation can be quite different from English. Here's a guide to the sounds of Arabic vowels:


  • ا - pronounced like the 'a' in "father"

  • ب - pronounced like the 'b' in "bat"

  • و - pronounced like the 'oo' in "food"

  • ي - pronounced like the 'ee' in "see"

  • ه - pronounced like the 'h' in "hello"
