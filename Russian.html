This webpage is designed for beginners who want to learn essential Russian phrases. You will find common phrases with audio that you can practice. Click the audio buttons to hear the correct pronunciation!

Common Phrases:

1. Здравствуйте - Hello (Zdravstvuyte)

2. Спасибо - Thank you (Spasibo)

3. До свидания - Goodbye (Do svidaniya)

4. Пожалуйста - You're welcome / Please (Pozhaluysta)

5. Да - Yes (Da)

6. Нет - No (Net)

7. Добрый вечер - Good evening (Dobryy vecher)

8. Очень приятно - Nice to meet you (Ochen' priyatno)

9. Меня зовут - My name is... (Menya zovut)

10. Я не понимаю - I don't understand (Ya ne ponimayu)

Pronunciation Tips:

In Portuguese, pronunciation can be straightforward with a few key tips to help you sound more natural.

  • R sounds: The 'r' in Portuguese can vary depending on the region. It may be rolled lightly or pronounced as a guttural sound.

  • Stress and vowels: Stress usually falls on the second-to-last syllable unless there is an accent mark. Vowels are pronounced clearly and distinctly.

    • tarde → TAR-de
    • bom → BONG

  • Vowel combinations:

    • Ã is pronounced like the 'a' in "sang" (nasalized).
    • É is pronounced like the 'e' in "bed".
    • Ô is pronounced like the 'o' in "more".

  • Consonants:

    • LH is pronounced like the 'lli' in "million" (e.g., filho → FEE-lyoo).
    • NH is pronounced like the 'ny' in "canyon" (e.g., manhã → ma-NYAH).
