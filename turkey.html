Turkish for Beginners

Welcome to Turkish for Beginners!

In this section, we will cover some basic Turkish phrases that are useful for everyday conversations. Listen to the pronunciation by clicking on the audio buttons next to each phrase.

Basic Phrases:

1. Merhaba - Hello

2. Nasılsınız? (Formal) / Nasılsın? (Informal) - How are you?

3. Günaydın - Good morning

4. Teşekkür ederim - Thank you

5. Lütfen - Please

6. Hoşça kal - Goodbye

7. Kaç yaşındasınız? (Formal) / Kaç yaşındasın? (Informal) - How old are you?

8. Adım... - My name is...

9. Tuvalet nerede? - Where is the bathroom?

10. Ne kadar? - How much does it cost?

Pronunciation Tips:

In Turkish, pronunciation is relatively straightforward. However, some letters are pronounced differently than in English. Here are some key tips:


  • Ç - Pronounced like "ch" in "chocolate" (e.g., çay - tea)
  • Ğ - A soft "g" sound that lengthens the preceding vowel (e.g., yağmur - rain)
  • İ - Pronounced like "ee" in "see" (e.g., İstanbul)
  • Ö - Similar to "i" in "bird" (e.g., güzel - beautiful)
  • Ş - Pronounced like "sh" in "shoe" (e.g., şarkı - song)

Additional Vocabulary:

  • Evet - Yes
  • Hayır - No
  • Şu an saat kaç? - What time is it?
  • Özür dilerim - I'm sorry
  • Adınız ne? - What is your name?
  • Tanıştığımıza memnun oldum - Nice to meet you
