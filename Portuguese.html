Portuguese Pronunciation Tips

Welcome to Portuguese Pronunciation Tips!

This webpage is designed to help you master the pronunciation of essential Portuguese words and phrases. You will find tips and audio examples to practice. Click the audio buttons to hear the correct pronunciation!

Pronunciation Tips:

1. Olá - Pronounced as "oh-LAH" (Hello)

2. Obrigada - Pronounced as "oh-bree-GAH-dah" (Thank you)

3. Adeus - Pronounced as "ah-DEH-oosh" (Goodbye)

4. De nada - Pronounced as "deh NAH-dah" (You're welcome)

5. Sim - Pronounced as "seen" (Yes)

6. Não - Pronounced as "nowng" (No)

7. Boa noite - Pronounced as "BOH-ah NOY-chee" (Good evening)

8. Prazer - Pronounced as "prah-ZEHR" (Nice to meet you)

9. Meu nome é... - Pronounced as "meh-oo NOH-mee eh" (My name is...)

10. Não entendo - Pronounced as "nowng en-TEN-doo" (I don't understand)

Pronunciation Tips:

In Portuguese, pronunciation can be straightforward with a few key tips to help you sound more natural.

  • R sounds: The 'r' in Portuguese can vary depending on the region. It may be rolled lightly or pronounced as a guttural sound.

  • Stress and vowels: Stress usually falls on the second-to-last syllable unless there is an accent mark. Vowels are pronounced clearly and distinctly.

    • tarde → TAR-de
    • bom → BONG

  • Vowel combinations:

    • Ã is pronounced like the 'a' in "sang" (nasalized).
    • É is pronounced like the 'e' in "bed".
    • Ô is pronounced like the 'o' in "more".

  • Consonants:

    • LH is pronounced like the 'lli' in "million" (e.g., filho → FEE-lyoo).
    • NH is pronounced like the 'ny' in "canyon" (e.g., manhã → ma-NYAH).
